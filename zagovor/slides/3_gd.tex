\begin{frame}{\color{aoblue}Gradientni spust}
\note{

}
Optimizacija z gradientnim spustom:
    $$
    \phi_{i+1} = \phi_i - \eta \nabla \loss(\phi_i) 
    $$
    Enačba gradientnega toka:
    $$
    \frac{d\phi}{dt} = - \nabla \loss(\phi), \quad \phi(0) = \phi_0
    $$
\end{frame}
